## Content Creation Guidelines

### Article Length
- **News/Updates**: 300-500 words
- **Analysis**: 800-1,200 words
- **Features**: 1,500-2,500 words
- **Guides**: 2,000-3,000 words

### SEO Best Practices
- **Title**: 50-60 characters, include main keyword
- **Meta Description**: 150-160 characters, compelling CTA
- **Excerpt**: 150-160 characters, summarize key points
- **Headings**: Use H2 for main sections, H3 for subsections
- **Keywords**: Include 2-3 times naturally throughout
- **Internal Links**: 2-3 relevant links per article
- **Images**: 1-2 high-quality images with alt text

### Writing Style
- Clear and concise language
- Active voice preferred
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Engaging headlines
- Compelling introductions
- Strong conclusions

### Fact-Checking
- Verify all statistics
- Cite reliable sources
- Check player information
- Confirm match results
- Validate quotes
- Update outdated information
